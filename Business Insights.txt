WALMART SALES ANALYSIS — KEY INSIGHTS

1.REVENUE INSIGHTS:

Total revenue across all branches: $322,966.75.
Branch C is the top revenue generator ($110,568.71), outperforming A and B.
Branches A and B are almost identical in revenue, differing by only $2.70.

2.BRANCH PERFORMANCE:

Branch C dominates in both revenue AND customer satisfaction (7.07 rating).
Branch A sells the most units (1,859) but ranks 2nd in revenue. 
Branch B is the weakest branch across revenue, units sold, and customer rating.

3.PRODUCT INSIGHTS:

Electronic Accessories is the #1 selling category (971 units).
Health & Beauty is the lowest performing category (854 units) — 12% below top seller.

4.PAYMENT BEHAVIOR:

Ewallet is the most preferred method by transaction count (345 transactions).
Cash generates the most revenue — meaning high-value purchases are made in cash.
Credit Card is the least used method (311 transactions, $100,767 revenue).

5.CUSTOMER INSIGHTS:

Member customers generate 3.5% more revenue than Normal customers.
Branch B's low rating (6.82) likely impacts repeat customer visits and long-term revenue.

STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS:

Branch C leads in revenue and customer satisfaction.
Branch B underperforms across all metrics — needs immediate attention.
Health & Beauty category requires promotional push.
Ewallet is most used, but Cash generates highest revenue.
Loyalty members spend more — expand the membership program.
